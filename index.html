About Candace

Hi, I'm Candace and I'm a developer who tells stories through code. I first fell in love with storytelling during my college years where I majored in Journalism. Over time, I’ve spread my wings from storytelling with words to images and video. I’ll never forget the first feeling of picking up a camera and pressing record, knowing I can share something special and unique with other people.

One day, I decided to start a blog but quickly realized some limitations in not knowing how to code. I decided to enroll in a web development program where I learned how to build, test and solve real problems through my coding projects. As a creative person, I enjoy using code to not only build functional projects but to reach people in a unique and engaging way. In my spare time, I love walking around taking photos, watching scary movies and going to meet-ups to catch up with friends and meet new people.

Skills include HTML, CSS, Javascript/jQuery, PHP/MySQL, Command Line, mobile web development and responsive design.
